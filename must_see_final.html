Must-See Places in India

From floating lakes to spiritual beach towns, explore these lesser-known destinations that deserve a spot on your bucket list.

Hidden Gems of India

Discover India's best-kept secrets and offbeat destinations

Majuli Island, Assam

The world's largest river island, known for its vibrant Vaishnavite culture, monasteries, and serene landscapes.

Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh

Famous for its lush green fields, rolling hills, and the unique Apatani tribal culture.

Gokarna, Karnataka

A hidden paradise with pristine beaches, yoga retreats, and a peaceful spiritual vibe.

Loktak Lake, Manipur

Home to the world's only floating national park with unique circular floating islands called "Phumdis".

Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh

Known for snow-capped mountains and India's largest Buddhist monastery — a serene cultural haven.
