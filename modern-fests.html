Modern/Contemporary Festivals

These festivals reflect the evolving interests of today’s generation, often blending entertainment, innovation, and global influences with India’s diverse cultural backdrop.

International and Global Festivals

These large-scale events showcase music, dance, and global cultures, drawing international artists and enthusiastic crowds to celebrate modern entertainment.

Goa Carnival – A vibrant fusion of music, dance, and parades rooted in Portuguese traditions.
Sunburn Festival – Asia’s premier EDM festival attracting top global DJs and fans.
NH7 Weekender – Celebrating indie and electronic music across major Indian cities.

Film Festivals

These cinematic gatherings bring together filmmakers, critics, and fans to celebrate Indian and international storytelling through powerful visuals and narratives.

IFFI – Goa’s International Film Festival showcasing global cinema excellence.
MAMI – Mumbai’s iconic film festival for indie and international films.

Regional Festivals

These events set the stage for India’s fashion revolution, highlighting top designers, runway trends, and global fashion innovation.

India Fashion Week – A showcase of Indian fashion’s diversity and creativity.
Lakme Fashion Week – Mumbai’s glamorous hub for fashion influencers and designers.

Technology and Innovation Festivals

These festivals promote cutting-edge tech, entrepreneurship, and futuristic innovations, attracting students, professionals, and creators from across the world.

IIT Techfest – Showcasing robotics, AI, and futuristic tech at IIT Bombay.
TechSparks – Bangalore’s top startup and innovation summit.
Bengaluru Space Expo – Celebrating India's growing space technology sector.
